Frequently Asked Questions

How do credits work?
Each text explanation costs 1 credit. Generating a visual with your explanation costs 10 credits total. New users start with 10 free credits.

Do credits expire?
No, your credits never expire.

How do I delete my account?
You can delete your account from within the app. Go to the Credits screen and tap "Delete Account" at the bottom.

Can I get a refund?
Credit purchases are non-refundable. Please contact Apple Support for any billing issues related to in-app purchases.
