Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2, 2025

Introduction

Smorts ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive a unique identifier for your account. If you choose to share your email address or name during sign-in, we store that information as well.

Usage Data: We track your credit balance and usage (number of explanations and visuals generated) to provide our service.

Content: The questions and topics you submit are processed to generate explanations and visuals. We do not permanently store the content of your queries.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To manage your account and credit balance
  • To process your in-app purchases
  • To improve our service

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple Sign In: For authentication
  • Google Cloud (Vertex AI): To process and generate explanations and visuals
  • Apple App Store: To process in-app purchases

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
